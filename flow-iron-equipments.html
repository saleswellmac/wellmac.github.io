Plug Valve

WellMac high pressure low torque Plug Valves are available in variety size & connections for easy and safe operations. They come in multiple bores and are available up to 15,000 PSI for standard service and 10,000 PSI for H2S (sour gas) service. Our plug valves are pressure balanced and have replaceable metal liners between the body and plug. Repair kits are also available to increase their life and make them perform safely

SWIVEL JOINT
